How it works

Three simple steps from booking to beautiful photos.

01

Book your date

Fill in our booking form with your wedding date and we'll confirm availability within 24 hours.

02

Receive the box

We ship all 10 cameras to you before your big day — pre-charged, ready to shoot, and easy to hand around.

03

Send them back

After the reception, drop the cameras back in the prepaid return box. We extract and deliver all photos digitally within 5 days.

The cameras

Small enough to fit in a pocket. Powerful enough to capture the moment.

1080P HD photos & video

Crisp, full HD resolution in every lighting condition — from golden-hour outdoor shots to the dance floor at midnight.

Thumb-sized & portable

Each camera is literally pocket-sized — small enough that guests barely notice they're holding one, until the perfect moment happens.

Built-in HD screen

A tiny built-in screen lets guests instantly review each shot — so they know they captured the moment before passing it on.

Pre-charged & ready

Every camera arrives fully charged before your big day — no fiddling, no dead batteries, no technical knowledge required.

One-click shooting

Dead-simple operation — a single button takes the photo. Any guest can pick one up and start shooting in seconds.

32 GB storage per camera

Enough for approximately 6,700 photos per camera. That's over 67,000 shots across all 10 cameras combined.

Your photos, your way

After the wedding, every shot from every camera lands in your own private portal.

01

Browse & curate

All photos from all 10 cameras are automatically collected and uploaded to your private gallery — sorted and ready to explore.

02

Delete what you don't want

Not every shot is a keeper. Easily remove the blurry, unflattering, or duplicate photos before anyone else sees them.

03

Download in full resolution

Save individual photos or the whole collection at full 1080P resolution — yours to keep, print, and treasure forever.

04

Share with your guests

Once you're happy with the collection, generate a share link and send it to everyone who attended. They can browse and download their favourites too.
